Creating your own defcards is easy. Just follow these simple steps:
  • Click the link below to create a card
  • Print out the page, cut the card out, and add some drawings or decorations if you want
  • Laminate the card (cheap and easy at any copy shop, such as Kinkos)
  • Leave the card somewhere others will find it
